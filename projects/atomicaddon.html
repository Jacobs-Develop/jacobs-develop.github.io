    Atomic Addon: In development

    Atomic Addon is another additional content for Core Mod.
    With this addon you can expand basic content of core mod that includes atomic core, atomic drill and liquid atom.
    Now you have acces to better and more advanced machines like atomic fuser, atom former and much more!
    And for those who want to have really dangerous machines, this addon add Atomic Reactor, very powerfull but
    also extremly dangerous.
